Simple Golf Handicap Data Entry System

How It Works!

Simply create an account using your email address.  You will get a verification notice.  Confirm your identity and you are ready to go.  Login to the site.  From there you can post your scores to track your handicap.  There are 3 levels of scoring to choose from: 1. Simple Net Score, 2.  Enter a score for each hole played or: 3. More detailed entry, where you also post fairways hit, GIR, penalty strokes.  

Review Your Work

After posting your scores you can review your scores or get detailed statistics on your performance.  The more scores you add the more analytics you have to see where you can improve your game.  For best results it is recommended that you keep track of all stats on your scorecard or by using the mobile app...
